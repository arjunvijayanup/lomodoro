Lomodoro

Privacy Policy — Last updated: 1 April 2026

Lomodoro does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

Local storage

All user preferences (timer durations, volume) are stored exclusively on your local device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API and are never sent to any server.

Audio stream

The extension connects to an audio stream provided by ilovemusic.de when you activate the lofi player. No user data is sent as part of this connection.

Permissions

Lomodoro requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — Saves timer durations, volume level, and session state locally on your device. No data leaves your machine.
  • alarms — Schedules a one-shot alarm to fire at the exact moment each session ends. Required because Chrome suspends service workers between events.
  • notifications — Displays a desktop notification when a work or break session ends, so you are alerted even when Chrome is not in focus.
  • offscreen — Creates a hidden background page to host the audio element. Required to keep the lofi stream playing after the popup is closed.
